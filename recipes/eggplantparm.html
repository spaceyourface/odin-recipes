Eggplant Parmigiana

This recipe will allow you to make an amazing Eggplant Parmigiana that will be sure to satisfy even the pickiest of eaters. Feel free to substitute any ingredients as you see fit.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sliced eggplant
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Make it
  2. Let it cool down
  3. Enjoy!
